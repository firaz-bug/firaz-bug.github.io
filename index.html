ABOUT

Sync is an intra-college symposium hosted by the Department of Computer Science. It is conducted with the notion of aggrandizing the intellect and kindling the passion for computer science in budding engineers. Sync promotes inter-department collaboration and interdisciplinary activities. Apart from technical events, it focuses on other nontechnical events to maintain the perfect equilibrium between tech and non-tech events.

Sync ‘21 is about to blossom afresh. Encompassing multifarious events, this version has the benchmark features to enthrall the multitude. Every experience brings a greater clarity of vision and thus with the past experiences, Sync ‘21 is all set to reach further horizons.

Events

TECH EVENTS


OLPC

A high-stakes fast-paced programming contest where the fastest minds compete to solve challenges in the most complicated data structures and algorithms. The participants set out to set new benchmarks and to compete both against the clock and other seasoned contestants.


CODING WITH THE STARS

A contest testing the persistence of the participants throughout a night where they solve a real-world challenge and show off their tech skills to impress a seasoned jury. The problem statement, the approach to solving it and the effectiveness of the prototype will be put to test in the early morning when the participants reap the harvest they nurtured through the night.


CODE FOR QUEENS

An event, exclusive for the cinderellas of CEG to showcase their tech strengths to grab the crown. The contestants are put up with a unique combination of math, puzzles, aptitude, and coding, and a clock ticking in the background, and compete to win the race towards the tiara.


HELLO WORLD

Geeks from Freshman year start exploring the obstacles in simple arithmetic, logic, and programming concepts. The participants’ ability to build primitive algorithms to hack the repetitive or iterative mathematical calculations will be tested. This event aims to lay a firm foundation in introducing them to the coding world.


HASH BROWN

The Sophomore nerds race among them to analyze, code, decode, debug and reverse engineer programs to uncover hidden mysteries and solve code obfuscations. The agility of the participants to solve the challenges in various forms is tested rigorously to find out that versatile problem solver.

NON-TECH EVENTS


WILD WORD CHASE

Come with a team of 2-3 and witness a fun, engaging, completely chaotic word game.Can blast all the fast-paced rounds with your word wits and fight your way to the top?!


KOLLYWOOD QUIZ

A fun event where the die-hard Kollywood Kannis (nerds) race against each other to answer questions on one of the greatest film industries to celebrate legends who have seen stages ranging from Filmfare to Oscars. ‘Devil is in the Details’ is still an understatement in the scope of this event.


SNAP HUNT

A super exciting event where the participants are made to search for locations inside our campus as the answers for an interesting puzzle. Put your CEG campus knowledge to use to win exciting prizes!
